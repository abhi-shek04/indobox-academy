Our Pathway

Your Pathway to Japan

A clear, step-by-step progression from intermediate language learning to corporate readiness and job placement.

1
Fluency

Step 1: N4 Career Foundation Course

Develop speaking speed, listening reflexes, and practical sentence structures. Prepare for intermediate daily interactions and initial career evaluations.

  • Interactive face-to-face roleplay and interview readiness
  • Build vocabulary (~600 Kanji, 1,500 words) for natural fluency
2
Mastery

Step 2: N3 Japan Career Course

Master polite speech (Keigo), complex business manners (like Meishi Koukan), and formal reading/writing skills required for Japanese workplace settings.

  • Bowing styles, professional greetings, and corporate culture
  • Advanced listening mock exams and formal workplace terminology
3
Outcome

Step 3: Secure Job Placements

Access a direct placement pipeline with Japanese tech employers via our partner IndiGate recruitment platform, completing your training-to-career pipeline.

  • Resume writing and specialized portfolio counseling
  • Direct placement facilitation matching you to jobs in Tokyo or Osaka
